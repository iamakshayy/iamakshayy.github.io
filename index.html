Hello! My name is Akshay and I enjoy designing products/tools that help the employees/customers. My interest in web development and designing started back in 2013 when I decided to take up Computer Science Engineering! Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at Amazon. My main focus these days is designing accessible, inclusive and meaningful products and doing user research at Amazon for a team called People eXperience and Technology (PXT) Solutions. My responsibilty includes doing user research, building an information architecture, producing low - fidelity wireframes and top quality high - fidelity wireframes, prototyping the wireframes and doing an accesibility check and user testing, before handing over the design files to the development team.

I'm a certified UI/UX Designer. I've completed the, UI/UX Specialization course on Coursera offered by California Institute of the Arts. In this Specialization, I summarized and demonstrated all stages of the UI/UX development process, and reviewed current best practices in UX design to create effective and compelling screen-based experiences for websites or apps. I completed a series of short exercises, both visual and non-visual, to apply acquired knowledge, from organizing and structuring screen-based content, defining goals and strategy, to creating wireframes and visual mockups. Finally, I produced a mid-level digital prototype with simulated functionality, as well as a comprehensive plan for a complex website.
